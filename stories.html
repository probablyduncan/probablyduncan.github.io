About

I'm Duncan Petrie. I come from Milwaukee, Wisconsin, USA, but I'm currently in Falmouth, Cornwall, UK. I've been writing all my life, and shooting since 2016. I built this site myself in the fall of 2019. Here, I maintain the best of my work. Look here for a look into my process, or here for my artist statement. For prints, business inquiries, questions, and more, contact me through email at duncanpetrie1@gmail.com, or Instagram at @probablyduncan.
